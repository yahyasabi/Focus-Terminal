# Focus-Terminal
ever wondered how games attract you when losing and re trying well they use an algorithm to trick your brain and give high doses of dopamine, thought about this and asked why not use this for my own benefits particularly studying so i built this app and the main idea is that when you die in-game you should go to this app (pro tip : make it as a widget) and you got to do one of the tasks to earn xp, if you accumulate enough you can respawn and re try and you even have a shop system, levels , and pomodro to track your time, another pro tip is that you should put your books that you will study at your ease of access so you wont get lazy etc.., hopefully it helps just like it did to me and ty
